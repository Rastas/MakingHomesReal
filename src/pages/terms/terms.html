TERMS & CONDITIONS

  1. Each day of the competition the SA Home Loans stand will be at a different location. Contestants must download the app, register and go this location.
  2. Daily prizes will be awarded to contestants who go to the location of the day, and scan the trigger image of the day at the SA Home Loans stand.
  3. This Trigger image will be located on a poster. Promoters will be available to guide you.
  4. The Daily Trigger image will unlock a piece of a house in the app. Contestants must add this piece to their app by scanning it in, and daily prize winners selected randomly from this pool of contestants.
  5. Daily prizewinners will be announced each afternoon that the competition runs.
  6. Daily prizewinners do not automatically get selected for the Grand Prize.
  7. Finalists for the R100 000 grand prize will be selected from contestants who go to at least 10 locations to add to their app.
  8. Contestants will be required to go to each activation point, on each day the competition is running, in order to scan the daily trigger image to complete their image of the house in the game.
  9. The collection of all 15 pieces of the house does not constitute a winner, but a finalist.
  10. On the final day of the competition (Friday 22 September 2017), a random selection of 3 finalists will be contacted in order to compete in a live 3D jigsaw house building competition on Saturday the 23rd of September 2017. Failure to be able to attend this event means forfeiture of the chance to compete for the R100 000 Grand Prize.
    11. The 3 Finalists may bring in 3 other family members or friends to help build the structure. They will be required to build a real 3D jigsaw house/ structure at finale of the competition. The team who completes this task first and to the satisfaction of the judges will win the R100 000 Grand prize. The remaining finalists will win consolation cash Prize of R10 000 each.
  11. SA Homeloan employees, their respective spouses, life partners and immediate family and their agencies are specifically excluded from entering this competition
  12. Apple is not a participant in or sponsor of this promotion.
